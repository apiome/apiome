Customer

Commerce Approved

Glossary Term · Business Glossary

Definition

A Customer is a person or legal entity that has completed at least one purchase transaction and has an active account in the platform. Distinct from a Prospect (pre-purchase, no completed transaction) and a Contact (general stakeholder without purchase history). A customer may hold one or more subscriptions and is always associated with a Tenant.

Synonyms

Client, Account Holder, End User

Related Terms

Account Prospect Subscriber Contact
