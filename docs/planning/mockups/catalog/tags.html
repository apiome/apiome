PII

Personally Identifiable Information

Sensitivity
Access Policy

Personally Identifiable Information (PII) is any data that could potentially identify a specific individual. Assets tagged PII require elevated access controls, audit logging, and must adhere to organizational data retention and deletion policies in compliance with GDPR and CCPA regulations.
