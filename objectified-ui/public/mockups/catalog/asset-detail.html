Customer

Data Object Commerce Q: 92
JS
Jane Smith · Data Steward · Product Team
· Updated 3 days ago

Description

Represents an individual or organization that has entered into a commercial relationship with the business. A Customer is created upon first successful purchase and maintains an active account in the platform. This is the central entity in the Commerce domain — all orders, subscriptions, and preferences are anchored to it.
