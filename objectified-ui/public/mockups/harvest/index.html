Harvest mockups

Enterprise systems accumulate thousands of undocumented internal services over time. Harvest solves the brownfield cataloging problem by automatically ingesting Express, FastAPI, Spring Boot, Django, and Rails codebases; reverse-engineering database DDL and PostgreSQL schemas; capturing live HTTP traffic; and parsing Postman collections and HAR files — then turning everything into structured Objectified specs and OpenAPI 3.1 documents ready for versioning, diffing, and publishing.
